Dealer Management System

Development of a multi-faceted platform enabling efficient invoice processing, credit note management, discount price protection, and AR aging analysis.

Invoice Management: "The system allows users to create, view, and manage dealer invoices, including details such as invoice number, date, nominal value, and status.Users can also generate and upload PDF versions of invoices.

Credit Note Processing: "BMUApps facilitates the creation and management of credit notes, enabling users to track relevant information and link them to invoices."

Discount Price Protection: "The platform includes a module for managing discount price protection, ensuring accurate tracking and reporting of price adjustments."

AR Aging Analysis: "The AR aging functionality provides users with insights into outstanding payments, including due dates, settlement amounts, and total outstanding balances."

User Authentication: "The system incorporates user authentication to ensure secure access, with features for password changes and sign-out."

News and Information: "The application also delivers news and updates to users."

Building Management System

Development of a resident portal providing features such as facility booking, digital forms, parcel management, ticket submission, announcements, and billing management.

User Authentication: "Residents can create accounts, log in securely, and manage their profiles, including password changes."

Estate Information: "The application provides residents with access to important information about their estate, such as announcements, events, and documents (e.g., house rules, facility terms)."

Facility Booking: "Residents can book facilities such as tennis courts and EV charging stations, view booking availability, and manage their reservations."

Digital Forms: "The application allows residents to submit various service requests and applications through digital forms, such as access card requests, moving out notices, and renovation requests."

Parcel Management: "Residents are notified of delivered parcels and can track their collection status."

Ticket/Issue Tracking: "Residents can report issues or maintenance requests, track their status, and communicate with management."

Billing and Payments: "Residents can view their current bills, payment history, and make online payments."

Community Features: "The application includes social features to foster community interaction, such as a community chatterbox for discussions and finding buddies."

Notifications: "Residents receive important notifications from the management."

Emergency Contacts: "The application provides a directory of important contacts, including emergency services."
